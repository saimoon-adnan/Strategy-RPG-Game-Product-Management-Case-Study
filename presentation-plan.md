# Data-Driven Presentation Plan  
Strategy RPG Game – Product Management Case Study

## Introduction

This document outlines a structured action plan for creating a **data-driven product presentation** for a Strategy RPG mobile game. The objective of this presentation is to analyze product performance, identify key business insights, and recommend strategies for improving engagement, retention, and monetization.

The presentation follows a step-by-step product management approach to ensure that insights are clear, data-driven, and actionable.

---

# Step 1: Define the Presentation Objective

The first step is to clearly define the purpose of the presentation.

### Key Questions
- What business problem are we trying to solve?
- What decision should stakeholders make after the presentation?
- What metrics will help explain the problem?

### Example Objective

Evaluate the performance of a strategy RPG mobile game and identify opportunities to improve **player engagement, retention, and revenue generation**.

---

# Step 2: Understand the Product

Before analyzing data, it is important to understand the core aspects of the game.

### Areas to Analyze

- Game genre and gameplay mechanics  
- Target audience  
- Monetization model  
- Core gameplay features  

Understanding the product ensures that data analysis is aligned with the actual player experience.

---

# Step 3: Identify Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Selecting the correct KPIs is critical for measuring product performance.

### Engagement Metrics
- Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Average Session Time

### Retention Metrics
- Day 1 Retention
- Day 7 Retention
- Day 30 Retention

### Monetization Metrics
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Conversion Rate (Free → Paying Users)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)

These KPIs help evaluate how effectively the game attracts, retains, and monetizes players.

---

# Step 4: Collect and Organize Data

The next step is gathering relevant data to analyze the game's performance.

### Data Sources

- Game analytics dashboards  
- Player engagement reports  
- Revenue and monetization data  

The collected data should be organized in a structured format to support clear analysis.

---

# Step 5: Analyze the Data

Data analysis helps identify patterns and performance gaps.

### Key Analysis Goals

- Identify trends in player engagement
- Evaluate retention performance
- Understand revenue performance

This step converts raw data into meaningful insights that can guide product decisions.

---

# Step 6: Generate Key Insights

After analyzing the data, the next step is to summarize the most important findings.

### Examples of Insights

- Player engagement may decline after the first few days
- Retention may be affected by onboarding experience
- Revenue may depend heavily on a small percentage of paying users

These insights help identify areas where the product can improve.

---

# Step 7: Develop Strategic Recommendations

Based on the insights, product managers must propose solutions.

### Example Recommendations

- Improve onboarding to increase Day 1 retention  
- Introduce new events or missions to improve engagement  
- Adjust in-game rewards to encourage player progression  

The goal is to provide actionable strategies that improve product performance.

---

# Step 8: Structure the Presentation

The presentation should follow a logical flow so stakeholders can easily understand the analysis.

### Suggested Slide Structure

1. Introduction  
2. Game Overview  
3. Business Problem  
4. KPI Framework  
5. Data Analysis  
6. Key Insights  
7. Strategic Recommendations  
8. Expected Impact  
9. Conclusion  

This structure ensures that the presentation clearly communicates the analysis and proposed strategies.

---

# Step 9: Design the Presentation

Visual clarity is important for communicating insights effectively.

### Best Practices

- Use charts and graphs for data visualization  
- Keep slides simple and easy to read  
- Highlight key metrics and insights  

Tools such as **PowerPoint, Google Slides, or Figma** can be used to create professional slides.

---

# Step 10: Review and Finalize

Before delivering the presentation, it should be reviewed carefully.

### Final Checks

- Verify data accuracy  
- Ensure slides follow a clear narrative  
- Confirm that recommendations are supported by data  

After these checks, the presentation is ready for delivery.

---

# Conclusion

A structured presentation plan ensures that product insights are communicated effectively to stakeholders.

By combining **clear objectives, relevant KPIs, data analysis, and strategic recommendations**, product managers can deliver presentations that support informed decision-making and improve product performance.
